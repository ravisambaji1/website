Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Dark Knight

A gripping crime thriller where Batman faces off against the chaotic genius of the Joker, played masterfully by Heath Ledger.

Inception

A mind-bending sci-fi heist film where dreams become the battleground for stealing and planting ideas.

The Godfather

A cinematic masterpiece that follows the rise of Michael Corleone in a powerful mafia family, blending crime, loyalty, and legacy.
